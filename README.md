Break the blocks in your path to make it to the right side of the screen in 5 seconds or less. Break flashing powerup blocks to increase your strength and break blocks faster.
Use arrow keys or WASD to move. Spam both at the same time to move faster. Sorry, this game is about spamming.
Strength and difficulty level perisist when quitting and restarting the game, unless you didn't make it to the goal on time during your last run.
